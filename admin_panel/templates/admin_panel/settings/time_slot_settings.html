Configuration Help
Slot Duration

Determines how long each appointment slot lasts. Common values are 30, 60, or 90 minutes.

Business Hours

Set your operating hours. Customers can only book appointments within these times.

Break Time

Optional lunch break or maintenance period. No appointments will be available during this time.

Advance Booking

How far in advance customers can book appointments. Prevents booking too far into the future.

Tips
  • Consider your service duration when setting slot length
  • Leave buffer time between appointments for cleanup
  • Break times help manage employee schedules
  • Advance booking limits prevent scheduling conflicts
